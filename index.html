About Us

DonorConnect is a platform connecting donors with registered NGOs to make in-kind donations simple and transparent. Our mission is to ensure every contribution reaches the right place at the right time.

  • Search NGOs by city and category and send structured requests.
  • Track the status of donations in real-time.
  • Tailored dashboards for donors, NGOs, and admins.

Our vision is to create a community of giving where helping others is simple and accountable.
